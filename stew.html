Potatoe-Stew

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch chunks
  • salt and fresh cracked pepper
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup Lake-town's finest red wine
  • 2 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
  • 4 carrots, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into 1 inch chunks
  • 5 cremini mushrooms, chopped
  • half an onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • chopped parsley

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, season the meat liberally with salt and pepper.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of flour toss to coat.
  3. Heat a skillet with a few tablespoons of vegetable oil.
  4. Add the meat and cook, stirring occasionally to brown on all sides.
  5. Once meat is brown, remove to the slow cooker
  6. Add potatoes, carrots, celery, mushrooms, onion, garlic and thyme on top of the meat.
  7. In the skillet, deglaze the pan with wine.
  8. Add 2 more tablespoons of flour and stir - it will seem like a thick paste.
  9. Add beef broth and heat until slightly thickened (lumps of flour are ok, they'll cook out in the slow cooker).
  10. Season with salt and pepper, add to the slow cooker, and give everything a good stir.
  11. Cook on low for 8 hours. When ready to eat, taste and adjust salt and pepper as needed, and mix in some fresh chopped parsley.
  12. Serve with some crusty bread and a pint of Barliman's Best.
